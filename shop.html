Coffee Stand Collection

We always believe in designing and crafting an item with in mind a classic aesthetic to last a lifetime and high utility. Our Pour Over stand collection respects the traditional Japanese method of making coffee with a modern design. Slowly pouring the water by hand over your coffee grinds results in the extraction of more flavour. This is the JM&Sons take on the pour over coffee stand, handcrafted and built with our one-of-a-kind, salvaged barn wood.
